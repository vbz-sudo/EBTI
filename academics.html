Academics at Ebenezer

We offer a Two-Week Intensive Bible Training Program, held three times annually (April, July, and December). Participants receive hands-on, practical instruction aimed at enhancing ministry competence and confidence. Graduates are awarded an Advanced Certificate in Biblical Studies & Leadership.
