Admissions

Join a Christ-centered community committed to biblical excellence and spiritual growth.

Admission Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • Certified copies of academic transcripts
  • Recommendation letter from a pastor or church leader
  • Two recent passport-size photos
  • Proof of payment for application fee

How to Apply

1. Prepare Documents

Gather all the required documents listed above before applying.

2. Fill Out the Form

Use our online application form below or visit our campus for assistance.

3. Submit and Wait for Response

After submission, our admissions team will contact you within 7 working days.
